About Us

Founded in 2023 by Hari K, Jamaav Corporation is a bootstrapped startup headquartered in Navi Mumbai, with an expanding presence in the United States. In just a short span, the company has demonstrated steady growth, achieving annual revenues exceeding $50,000 since inception. Driven by innovation, integrity, and impact, Jamaav continues to empower businesses through technology-led transformations and strategic partnerships worldwide.

  • Global Presence: Bharat & United States
